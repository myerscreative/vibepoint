# VibePoint — FAQ

## Is VibePoint a therapy tool?
No. VibePoint is a self-awareness and performance tool for understanding emotional patterns.

## What makes it different from other mood trackers?
VibePoint doesn't track moods — it breaks emotions into ingredients so users can change them.

## Do I need to journal?
No journaling. No paragraphs.  
Just simple entries that reveal patterns.

## Is my data safe?
Yes. We do not sell or share data. Everything is encrypted.

## What age group is this for?
Ages 13+.  
But adults benefit the most.

## Can VibePoint help with anxiety?
VibePoint is not a treatment tool.  
It is a clarity and state-awareness tool that can support emotional understanding.
