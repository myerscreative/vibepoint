# VibePoint — Brand Voice Guide

## Tone Pillars
- Direct  
- Empowering  
- Clear  
- Action-oriented  
- Non-clinical  
- No “victim language”  

## Style Rules
- Keep sentences strong and short  
- Use clarity over complexity  
- Avoid emotional labels; focus on ingredients  
- Promote responsibility, not rumination  

## Vocabulary to Use
- State  
- Pattern  
- Clarity  
- Choice  
- Ingredients  
- Shift  
- Power  
- Awareness  

## Vocabulary to Avoid
- Disorder  
- Symptom  
- Diagnosis  
- Journaling  
- Coping  
- Struggle  

## Guiding Belief
Emotional mastery is available to anyone willing to build awareness and take control of the ingredients behind their states.
